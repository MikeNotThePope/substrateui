Add themable "feel" tokens and make the lava theme molten. New structural tokens `--motion-duration`, `--motion-ease`, and `--radius-factor` let themes vary motion timing and corner softness, not just color. Defaults are identical to previous rendering. Lava now overrides all three — slower viscous ease-out motion (300ms), corners scaled 1.5x, and hard shadows tinted deep magma instead of gray — so it feels molten rather than merely recolored.
